Welcome to M CHEREVILLE HOTEL

This is my hotel…….exclusively!
M Chereville Hotel, the newest business- boutique hotel with five star hotel standards open its door last February 2009. The newly opened lounging destination perfectly melds the grandeur of world-class accommodation with value for money.

M. Chereville Hotel caters specially to discerning guests who value privacy and relaxation – quite ideal for both foreign and local businessmen looking for a hotel that provides the most basic amenities yet offers that extra touch of grandeur amidst its simplicity and functionality. The rooms are amazingly luxurious and can equal a five-star hotel accommodation. The features are simply out of the ordinary.

Aside from its innate elegance, M Chereville Hotel features its own Duty Free Shop at the ground floor by the lobby area. Well-appointed guest rooms and suites maintain the "consistency of irregularities" as an art form where furniture of different sizes and hues are mixed to form a kind of " art noveau".

For the finest dining experience, M Cafe Restaurant an exclusive haven for guest offers international cuisines . Complimenting the ambiance is the soothing music that will enchant diners. As you cap the day, get back that lively feeling and de-stress yourself at the I Park Garden Bar and Grill with the pulsating music nightly as we enliven more the guests with its various music offerings that will excite more the evenings.

We at M Chereville Hotel cordially invites you to have this unique experience...exclusively!
